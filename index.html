About Bill Processor™

Bill Processor™ optimizes your financial workflows. Integrated directly into your Google Workspace™ environment, our tool empowers users to seamlessly organize, analyze, and process statement documents, receipts, and invoices without leaving their cloud environment.

Application Setup Guide

Follow these instructions to configure Bill Processor™ after installation:

  1. Install the App: Click the "Install" button on the Google Workspace Marketplace™ listing page.
  2. Grant Permissions: Review and accept the OAuth authorization prompts to securely connect your workspace pipelines.
  3. Initialize Integration: Open your Google Workspace™ container (such as Google Sheets™ or the sidebar layout), find the Bill Processor™ icon, and start scanning your financial layouts instantly.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how Bill Processor™ handles user data. Our core data principle is absolute transparency and minimal data acquisition.

1. Data Collection and Usage

Bill Processor™ explicitly limits data interaction. The application only accesses data endpoints dynamically granted by the user during explicit operational actions. We do not run background scraping processes on your system files.

2. Data Sharing and Transfer

We strictly prohibit the sale, external transfer, or commercial monetization of user data. All processing operations occur locally or directly within authenticated secure parameters of your Google Cloud™ project scopes. No information is passed to third-party advertising brokers.

3. Data Retention and Deletion

We do not maintain independent databases to store persistent user data records. Session tokens and operational keys are temporary and dynamically managed. Users can instantly revoke authentication access permissions at any time via their standard Google Account security panel settings.
